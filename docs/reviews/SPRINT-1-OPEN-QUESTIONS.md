## Explicitly deferred product scope

Custom pace selection, bookmarks, badges/achievements, leaderboards/social challenges, institutional account roles, payments, live AI generation, pronunciation scoring, speech analysis, and live tutoring are post-MVP. They are not open MVP implementation choices.

## Resolved boundaries retained

- Learner-visible content is an approved, published Lesson Revision.
- Tutorial and application-silent practice are separate; only eligible practice completes a lesson.
- Launch languages are English, isiZulu, and Sepedi; pace presets are Easy/Medium/Hard at 100/150/200 WPM.
- Packages use the approved five textual block types, zero-based Revision-scoped Reading Positions, versioned profiles, and SHA-256 integrity.
- Revision identity, Package Checksum, Asset Checksum, and Transport URL remain distinct.
- Sync supports per-event accepted, duplicate, rejected, and retryable outcomes without silent loss.
- History-safe deletion prevents late Devices/outbox events from restoring identity.

The [Sprint 2 Entry Checklist](./SPRINT-2-ENTRY-CHECKLIST.md) is the execution gate for the Sprint 2 items above.
